Barbeque Grilled Chicken Pizza

Description

Quick and easy for those summer cookouts. I often make as an appetizer while the ribs are cooking. Use whatever toppings you normally like on regular pizza, but the barbeque chicken is always a crowd pleaser!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup chopped chicken
  • 1/2 cup barbeque sauce, divided
  • 1 (13.8 ounce) package refrigerated pizza
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil, or as needed
  • 1 cup shredded Colby-Monterey Jack cheese
  • 1 cup diced tomatoes, or to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat grill
  2. Mix chickenand 2 tablespoons barbeque sauce
  3. Roll dough onto a work surface and cut in half
  4. Place dough onto the grill
  5. Spread the remaining barbeque sauce
  6. Cook pizza until cheese is melted
